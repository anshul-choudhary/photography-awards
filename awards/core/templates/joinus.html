How to register for award

To participate you should:

  1. Register here
  2. Pay Award Entry Fee
  3. Upload five photos

Wedisson Award is given to outstanding, amazing, stunning wedding photography. Only few photos are selected by our curators. We accept entries for Award every month and choose only 30 - 60 photographs.

Deadline for submissions:

  • 13 Awards Submission - March 3 to March 31

FAQ

How can I advertise as a photographer?

If 3 of your images win the award (calculated among all awards) you will be granted a personal page for 1 year.

I can't find my city in the list of cities during registration.

You can choose any other city in your country and register. After that you can e-mail us at info@wedisson.com and we will add your city to our database, you can change it in your profile anytime.

How do I know that my photos are submitted?

After you pay fee and upload minimum 3 images then the 'submit' button appears. You can also delete or change any image you submitted before the deadline.

Do I need to resize the images so they fit within the Instagram 1:1 sizes?

No, photo should be 1200px wide, preferably horizontal as we publish them on our website with original size. For instagram account we resize them later.

How do I know that my photo is selected or not?

If photo is chosen during competition it appears here on the main page

How many times can I participate in the award?

You are recommended participating in Award every month because at the end of the year we count all the awards and make a rating of best photographers worldwide.

What about copyrights?

All uploaded photographs belong to their owners. We don't have the right and don't use it anywhere else, only on our website wedisson.com and on our instagram profile instagram.com/wedisson_com
